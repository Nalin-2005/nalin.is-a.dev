Notes-App

Nota - The Note Taking App

Nota is a simple and clean note taking app which gives you materially designed minimal note taking experience which helps you to stay focused.

Features
Note Space

Keeps all your notes at one place, makes it available right when you need them.

Data and Security

Notes [Notes Data] are saved on your Local Machine.

100% Secure

Your thoughts truely yours (No Cloud Connectivity)

Search Notes

Search by note title or by any word noted on any note

Sort Notes

Notes can be sorted by Alphabetically, Last Edited, or Recently Drafted

Delete Note

Deletes permanently.
