To-Do-App

ToDo App - Get things done.

As the name suggests, To-Do App is a clean, modular looking To-Do list making app.

Features
To-Do Space

Browse all your to-do's at one place, makes it available right when you need them.

Data and Security

All to-do's are saved on your Local Machine.

100% Secure

Your work, your business (No Cloud Connectivity)

Strike To-Do

Strike To-Do when done with your task.

Sorted To-Do

To-Do Default order set to First in First Order, manual priority control.

Delete To-Do

Deletes permanently.

How to use

1. In the textbox note your To-Do and hit enter to add it to your list.
2. Left click on the To-Do to mark it as done.
3. Right click on the To-Do to delete it.
